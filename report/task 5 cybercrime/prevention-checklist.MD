Stay Safe Online Checklist:

1. Do not share OTPs or passwords with anyone.

2. Always verify the sender’s email address.

3. Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links.

4. Use strong and unique passwords.

5. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).

6. Do not enter personal details on untrusted websites.

7. Be cautious of urgent or too-good-to-be-true offers.

8. For financial fraud, report immediately on cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930.
