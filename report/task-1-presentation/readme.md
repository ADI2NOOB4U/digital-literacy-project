In Task 1, I was asked to design an infographic on digital literacy using Canva.
The aim of designing this infographic was to represent what digital literacy is in simple and attractive ways. 
The information included in this infographic was basic and related to digital literacy. 
This included knowing what digital literacy is, internet safety, how to behave professionally on the internet, and digital responsibility. 
and how digital literacy is important in our lives.
The challenge I encountered while designing this infographic was how to include adequate information without overloading space. 
The importance of this task was to learn that digital literacy is not just knowing how to use digital tools, 
But it is also important to use them safely and responsibly.
